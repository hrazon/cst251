The purpose of this lab is to still utilize the CSS rules from previous labs and continue building on those. With an addition of adding a sidebar div and styling it the following:

  • It is inline-block style
  • Has a width of 1/3 of the page
  • Has a minimum width of 300px
  • Positioned to the right of the rest of the content
